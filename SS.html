"Sumo Soccer is a Rocket League-inspired soccer game in which players take on the role of sumo wrestlers who ground-pound to create shock waves to propel objects. The game is fast-paced and competitive, but also funny and entertaining. Within a round, there should be moments of high tension, where players are on the edge of their seats trying to score or defend, and moments of absurdness as both sumos and ball are sent flying in the air." --Sumo Soccer team

SOUND EFFECTS

"The music in Sumo Soccer, like the game itself, takes inspiration from the pounding electronic soundtrack of Rocket League, but infused in it are themes constructed using traditional Japanese scales such as the Hirajoshi and Insen scales." --Jason Ng, Composer
