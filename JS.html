"WELCOME TO JETSWAP"

"PREPARE TO SWAP"

"JetSwap is a king-of-the-hill style 4-player game with an object/player swapping mechanic. It can be thought of as 'reverse tag.' Instead of the tagged person chasing everyone, everyone hunts after and tries to swap with the player who has the crown. The aesthetic is inspired by a combination of the neo-noir cyberpunk Blade Runner and the metallic sci-fi look of Rocket League." --JetSwap team

"JETSWAP BLUES"

"AESCHYLUS I"

"JetSwap's music enhances and complements the most significant character in the game - the environment. With its combination of detuned chords, synth plucks, and acoustic instrumentation, the music was aimed at balancing the sound of the 80s cyberpunk, the neo-noir (Blade Runner, Only God Forgives), and contemporary mainstream action music." --Jason Ng, Composer
